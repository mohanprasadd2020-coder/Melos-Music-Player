## 🚀 How to Use (Right Now!)

### Access the App

Open your browser to: **http://localhost:8080/**

### Create Your First Playlist

1. Sidebar → Click **"Playlists"**
2. Click blue **"New"** button
3. Enter name and press Enter
4. Done! ✓

### Add Songs to Playlist

1. Search or browse for songs
2. Click **three-dot menu** (⋯) on any song
3. Select **"Add to Playlist"**
4. Choose your playlist
5. Song added! ✓

### Play a Playlist

1. Sidebar → Playlists → Click any playlist card
2. Click **"Play All"** button
3. All songs play in order ✓
